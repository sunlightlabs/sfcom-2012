Congress for your Android Phone!

'Congress' is a free pocket directory that allows you to quickly get detailed information on your members of Congress, and what they're up to. Follow the latest bills and laws, see recent votes, and read about your legislators in the news.

From the palm of their hands, concerned citizens can better engage with their government, and Capitol Hill regulars can finally ditch those hefty yellow books.

The application is built on the Sunlight Congress API and GovTrack.us. Like all Sunlight projects, 'Congress' is open source software, and we encourage developers to play and reuse the code on Github. For iPhone users, check out the Sunlight Foundation’s Real Time Congress application that allows users to access government documents and activity as they go live.

Computerworld named 'Congress' one of the '10 free Android apps for staying in the know'. The app has also been previously featured on the Android Market at large, and T-Mobile's Market channel.

Download the app from the Android Market, or scan this QR Code.

Features

  • Read the latest bills and laws, and see how the votes were cast.
  • Find and call your members of Congress by using your phone's location, a zipcode, a last name, a state, or by committee.
  • Real-time notifications of votes, laws, and all sorts of other Congressional activities.
  • View your lawmakers' latest votes, committee memberships, or a map of their district.
  • Read tweets and watch videos from lawmakers' Twitter and YouTube accounts, or follow them in the news.
  • Easily share interesting bills across your social networks.
  • Add favorite lawmakers or bills to your dashboard, or create shortcuts on your Android home screen.
