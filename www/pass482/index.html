WE NEED YOUR HELP

Candidates for the US Senate are the only federal candidates who fail to make their campaign finances available online in a timely fashion.
The Senate is considering a bill—S.482—that would fix this.

“Unfortunately, one senator, Sen. Roberts, KS, is setting this bill up to fail by tacking on an unnecessary and controversial amendment that would actually decrease transparency and accountability in the Senate.”

Fortunately, Helping is as Easy as These 3 Simple Steps.

  • Call your senators.
  • Ask your senators if they will co-sponsor S. 482.
  • Ask your senators to oppose the Roberts amendment.
